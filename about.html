About This Project

This Bus Route Finder project is developed as a university group project. The main goal of this system is to help people easily find bus routes by searching bus names or locations. This platform can save time, reduce confusion, and make daily commuting easier for city passengers.

Users can search specific bus names to know their routes, read informational blog articles about popular buses, and get an overall idea of city transportation in a simple and user-friendly way.
